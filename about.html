About Us

The Legacy that is The Mittal Group

Established in 2001, Mittal Group began its journey as a trusted name in the coal trading industry, delivering high-quality coal solutions to power industries across various sectors. With a strong foundation built on reliability and integrity, we earned a reputation for efficiency, customer-centric service, and sustainable practices.

In 2008, under the visionary leadership of Mr. Nitin Agrawal, we expanded our horizons and ventured into the world of real estate. Bringing the same principles of trust, excellence, and innovation, we have redefined urban living with landmark developments embodying luxury, quality, and thoughtful design.

From fueling industries to shaping skylines, Mittal Group continues its journey of transformation, committed to excellence in every endeavor.

Mr. Murlidhar Agrawal

Founder and Chairman

The journey of The Mittal Group began with the vision and determination of our founder, Mr. Murlidhar Agrawal. With an initial investment of just ₹50,000 in agricultural land, Mr. Agrawal laid the foundation of what would become a legacy. His instinctive knack for buying and selling land quickly became his second nature, which became an anchor for a remarkable story of growth and success. Over the years, Mr. Murlidhar Agrawal’s unwavering commitment established a solid foundation for the group, turning opportunities into achievements.

Mr. Nitin (Bunty) Agrawal

Managing Director

A new chapter began when Mr. Nitin Agrawal, driven by passion and a vision for Nagpur, infused fresh energy into the business. Expanding beyond its roots, he led The Mittal Group into coal trading, diversifying its portfolio and unlocking new opportunities. With a steadfast commitment to excellence, he stepped into real estate in 2008, laying the foundation for remarkable developments that continue to shape the city's landscape.
