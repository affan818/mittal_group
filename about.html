About Us

The legacy that is The Mittal Group

Mr. Murlidhar Agrawal

Founder and chairman

The journey of The Mittal Group began with the vision and determination of our founder, Mr. Murlidhar Agrawal. With an initial investment of just ₹50,000 in agricultural land, Mr. Agrawal laid the foundation of what would become a legacy. His instinctive knack for buying and selling land quickly became his second nature, which became an anchor for a remarkable story of growth and success. Over the years, Mr. Murlidhar Agrawal’s unwavering commitment established a solid foundation for the group, turning opportunities into achievements.

Mr. Nitin (Bunty) Agrawal

Managing Director

A new chapter unfolded when his 20-year-old son, Mr. Nitin Agrawal, brought fresh energy and vision into the business. Mr. Nitin Agrawal expanded the family’s horizons by venturing into coal trading, diversifying the group’s portfolio and opening doors to new opportunities. His leadership marked The Mittal Group’s entry into real estate in 2008 with the construction of 42 row houses, a milestone project that set the stage for future success. This was soon followed by the launch of the Skyline Project, further solidifying the group’s reputation in the real estate sector.
