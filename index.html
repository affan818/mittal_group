About Us

Since 2001, headed by Nitin Agrawal, the group has diversified businesses in Coal Trading, Transportation, Finance, Investment, and Education, delivering excellence and reliability for over two decades. Our commitment to quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction has positioned us as a preferred partner across industries.

Under the visionary leadership of our director, Mr. Nitin Agrawal, we have expanded our expertise beyond coal trading to include real estate ventures, catering to the evolving needs of businesses and investors. While coal trading remains our core strength - providing high-quality, timely, and competitively priced fuel solutions - we have also made significant strides in real estate development, offering premium commercial and residential properties.

At Mittal Group, we understand energy and infrastructure's critical role in economic growth. Our robust supply chain, deep industry expertise, and dedication to sustainable practices ensure that we continue creating lasting value. Our tailored financial solutions complement our services, helping businesses confidently navigate their investment needs.

Driven by trust, innovation, and a customer-first approach, Mittal Group remains committed to powering industries, building communities, and shaping a sustainable future.
